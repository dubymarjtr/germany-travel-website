Where to stay in Germany?

When traveling to a different country, looking at a place to stay may be hard to decide. Whether you stay at an Airbnb or a hotel, Germany has so many options for tourists to stay at all over in this country. Here are some of the best options to stay in Germany with a variety of different cities to stay in. Included are some of the features and prices that you would see if you stayed in these places.

Manor House - Gnevkow

  • The Manor House is very historic and a beautiful building. It is a very grand house that sleeps up to sixteen people and is still a comfortable living environment with central heating and other features. Lots of remodels have been done over the past eight years. This is a house that is great for a bigger group of travelers that is more affordable. The total cost will be just a little over $600 per night.

Spacious Villa - Schönfeld

  • This Airbnb consists of four apartments. Each one has a shower bath, bedroom, living room, and kitchen. If visiting with other fellow travelers, there are many shared areas in the house. The best part is the outside which includes a pool and a sauna. Originally built around 1900, many renovations have been done to make this a great place to stay at. The total cost is around $800 per night.
Visit Website

Private Luxury Chalet - Siegsdorf

  • This is a great place to travel to during the winter or summer months. It has a great atmosphere for a warm environment for guests that are looking for a staycation place in Germany. During the warmer months, this chalet has great walking trails to explore the great countryside of Germany. It sleeps up to eight guests and will be around $700 per night depending on the season.
Visit website


Hotel Deutsche Eiche

  • This wonderful hotel, restaurant, and bathhouse is located in Munich. It is in the heart of Munich which has a lot of great possibilities for going out. This hotel has many amenities such as paid private parking on-site, free Wi-Fi, a bar, sauna, dry cleaning, and even a rooftop terrace. The room itself contains air conditioning, a tv, refrigerator, housekeeping, and a minibar. This hotel is more cost-efficient and a room with one King bed will cost around $150 per night.
Visit Website

Hotel Adlon Kempinski

  • This beautiful hotel is located in Berlin. It was founded in 1907 and is right next to the Brandenburg gate. This hotel offers many amenities such as a pool, valet parking, free Wi-Fi, a bar, babysitting, fitness center, bicycle rental, and pets are allowed. The room itself contains the normal amenities such as room service, housekeeping, tv, minibar, air conditioning, and it even has a private balcony. The total cost per night is about $318 for an executive room with a King-sized bed.
Visit Website

Arabella Alpenhotel

  • This hotel is located in the mountains in Bavaria, Germany. It is a beautiful hotel that is at an altitude of 1,100 meters. Surrounding the mountains is a lake. This hotel is in the heart and nature of Germany. It offers amenities such as free parking, free breakfast, bicycle rental, free WiFi, and a fitness center. The room itself features housekeeping, room service, tv, and complimentary toiletries. For a double room with a King-sized bed, the cost per night would be around $226.
