Where to eat in Germany?

Germany is known for a lot of different culinary dishes, as well as for their alcoholic drinks, such as beer. The German food is something you have to experience while traveling here, and these restaurants below will delight you with such amazing food. Here is also included some fun coffee shops to grab a bite or have brunch, and a list of bars and clubs to go have fun at night.

Markthalle Neun

  • This great restaurant is located in Berlin, and it is one of the city’s first street markets. It was almost demolished but was saved and now has some of the best street food dishes around Germany. One of the featured items is Sironi’s Italian-German bread creations. And the prices are pretty budget-conscious for food in Germany.
Visit Website

Spezlwirtschaft

  • This is a great restaurant located in Munich. The building has great architecture and was built about 800 years ago. It has a contemporary atmosphere and good food. Try the doughy spinach dumplings and this restaurant is known for its alcoholic drinks. The price is about mid-range for restaurants in Germany.
Visit Website

Curry & Co

  • This restaurant is located in Dresden and is an authentic currywurst German restaurant. There are five types of currywurst sausages such as pork, beef, and vegan sausages. There are also different sauces to go along with your sausage but try the non-traditional honey-mustard. The prices are on a budget for your trip to Germany.
Visit website


Kin Dee

  • This restaurant is located in Berlin which serves Thai food with seasonal ingredients from Berlin. Chef Dalad Kambhu includes the flavors and taste from her childhood home in Thailand into German cuisine. Be sure to check out the confit octopus dish. This restaurant is more high-end, so the prices are more expensive.
Visit Website

The Barn

  • This is the leading coffee roaster all over Europe and has been growing since 2010. It has subscriptions for people who want to buy coffee, and it also has cafe locations all over Berlin. It has an urban feel to all the coffee shops and the architecture in each building is beautiful.
Visit Website

Man vs. Machine

  • This is one of the best coffee shops in all of Munich. This is not just a coffee shop, but it can also do wholesales and outdoor deliveries for special events. It has a great coffee delivery service that customers love.
Visit Website


Dauth Schneider

  • This bar is located in Frankfurt on a cobble-stoned street. This bar has a tavern feel to it. It opens at 11:30 am and closes at midnight. Be sure to try the signature drink, Ebbelwoi, which is a light alcoholic apple flavor.
Visit Website

Hofbräuhaus

  • This is a beer hub located in Munich. Munich is a city known for its clubs and nightlife. The beer at the Hofbräuhaus is served by Bavarian lasses and is a great place to explore at night. This bar has more of a traditional feel to it. It is open from 9 am till 11:30 pm.
Visit Website

Rudi

  • This bar is located in Leipzig, and it is a perfect place to go get cocktails. It is a trendy bar that serves small plate dishes along with great drinks. It also houses artwork around the entire building. The bar regularly changes up the artwork on display. The price of the drinks and food is mid-range.
