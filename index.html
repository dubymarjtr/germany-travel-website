Why Traveling to Germany?

Many countries might be in your travel list, but Germany has many reasons why it should be at the top! When visiting a new place, female travelers might have many concerns, but here is a list of the most basic reasons to pick Germany as your next travel destination:

Safety

Traveling can be hard and at times scary, especially for female solo travelers. In addition to all the increased protection of the German police force, the country has many safety features found throughout the cities. Apart from general precautions that everybody should follow, such as not walking alone at night in desert streets, you should not feel in danger while exploring this country. As a popular European destination, pickpockets are common, so be aware of this in public transportation, or crowded places.

History

If you love history, or even if you are not a big fan of museums and historical art, Germany would amaze you with an extensive history that can be found in every corner. During and after World War I, Germany was not that great of a country. Poverty was everywhere, and socioeconomic issues allowed Hitler to take power. After World War II, Germany remains destroyed, worst than ever. But history played it to its favor, and now it holds the title of the best country in the European Union.

Diversity

Diversity is everywhere and Germany has a positive atmosphere of inviting people from all over the world. Germany is known for welcoming immigrants and wanting them to achieve a better life. Some things that the German government did were simplify immigration papers, and even provide free German language classes (Faiola). Diversity is going to keep growing in Germany, and all tourists around the world are welcome to travel and explore in this country.
